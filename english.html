Profile

On the strength of our vast and solid experience in the employability field, we at Le Centre A.M.I. offer a large spectrum of bilingual services in two major domains: job search, and major employability events for people eager to find work. Throughout the years, Le Centre A.M.I.'s team have adapted itself to the new standards regulating the labour market. We are now better equipped to respond more adequately to the needs of each individual.
